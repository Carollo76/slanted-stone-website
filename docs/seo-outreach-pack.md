## 6. Step B5 — Tourism roundup / blogger pitch (ongoing)

Target writers of "best Poconos cabins," "Airbnbs with a sauna in PA," "large group cabins
in the Poconos," etc. Pitch the genuinely unique angles: **authentic Finnish barrel sauna**
and the **ski-lift chair swing**.

> **Subject:** A Poconos cabin with a real Finnish barrel sauna — for your roundup?
>
> Hi [name],
>
> Loved your piece on [best Poconos cabins / cabins with hot tubs]. If you ever refresh it,
> I think Slanted Stone Chalet would be a great add — it's one of the few Pocono Pines rentals
> with an **authentic Finnish barrel sauna** (plus a hot tub, a stone firepit, and an actual
> ski-lift chair swing in the yard). Sleeps 8, brand-new build.
>
> High-res photos and details here: https://slantedstone.com — happy to send anything you need.
>
> Thanks for considering it!
> [Your name]

**Where to find targets:** Google `best Poconos cabins`, `Poconos Airbnb with sauna`,
`large group cabins Poconos` → note the blogs/listicles that rank → find the author's email
or contact form. Also worth a free **HARO/Qwoted/Featured** account to catch "looking for
unique rentals" journalist requests.
